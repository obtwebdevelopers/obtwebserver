Photoshop

Photoshop is a graphics editor developed and published by Adobe Systems for Windows and OS X. Photoshop is considered to be one of the leaders in photo editing software. This software allows users to manipulate, crop, resize, and correct color on digital photos. This software is particularly popular amongst professional photographers and graphic designers.

It is used by photographers, graphic and web designers, videographers, and 3D artists to enhance and manipulate photos and create original digital art work.
